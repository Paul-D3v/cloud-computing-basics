# Creating Cloud Provider Free-Tier Accounts

## Overview

This guide walks you through creating free-tier accounts on the three major cloud providers:
- **Amazon Web Services (AWS)**
- **Google Cloud Platform (GCP)**
- **Microsoft Azure**

Each provider offers a free tier with credits and free services for learning and testing.

---

## AWS Free Tier Account

### What You Get:
- **$300 credit** for 12 months (new accounts)
- **12 months free** on select services:
  - 750 hours/month of EC2 t2.micro or t3.micro instances
  - 5GB of S3 storage
  - 750 hours/month of RDS database
  - 1 million Lambda requests/month
- **Always free** services:
  - 1 million Lambda requests/month (permanently)
  - 25GB DynamoDB storage
  - And more...

### Requirements:
- Valid email address
- Phone number for verification
- Credit/debit card (will NOT be charged for free tier)
- Government-issued ID (may be required)

### Step-by-Step Instructions:

**Step 1: Go to AWS**
- Visit: https://aws.amazon.com
- Click **"Create an AWS Account"** (top right)

**Step 2: Enter Account Information**
```
Email address: your-email@example.com
AWS account name: My AWS Account (or your company name)
```
- Click **"Verify email address"**
- Check your email and enter the verification code

**Step 3: Create Password**
```
Password: (Create a strong password)
Confirm password: (Re-enter password)
```
- Click **"Continue"**

**Step 4: Contact Information**
```
Account type: Personal (or Business if applicable)
Full name: Your Name
Phone number: +1234567890
Country/Region: United States (or your country)
Address: Your address
City: Your city
State: Your state
Postal code: Your ZIP code
```
- Agree to AWS Customer Agreement
- Click **"Continue"**

**Step 5: Payment Information**
```
Credit or debit card number: Your card number
Expiration date: MM/YY
Cardholder's name: Name on card
Billing address: (Should auto-fill from Step 4)
```
- Click **"Verify and Continue"**

⚠️ **Important**: AWS will place a temporary $1 authorization hold on your card to verify it. This will be refunded.

**Step 6: Identity Verification**
```
Choose verification method:
○ Text message (SMS)
○ Voice call

Enter security check code: (from image)
```
- Click **"Send SMS"** or **"Call me now"**
- Enter the 4-digit verification code you receive
- Click **"Continue"**

**Step 7: Select Support Plan**
```
Choose: Basic Support - Free
```
- This is sufficient for learning and free tier usage
- Click **"Complete sign up"**

**Step 8: Confirmation**
- You'll see: "Congratulations! Your AWS account is ready"
- Click **"Go to the AWS Management Console"**
- Sign in with your email and password

**Step 9: Set Up MFA (Recommended)**
- In the console, click your account name (top right)
- Click **"Security credentials"**
- Under **"Multi-factor authentication (MFA)"**, click **"Activate MFA"**
- Follow the instructions to set up authenticator app

### Important Notes:
- ⏰ **Monitor your usage**: Set up billing alerts to avoid unexpected charges
- 📧 **Verify email**: Make sure to verify your email address
- 🔒 **Secure your account**: Enable MFA immediately
- 📊 **Free Tier Dashboard**: Check https://console.aws.amazon.com/billing/home#/freetier

## Google Cloud Platform (GCP) Free Tier Account

### What You Get:
- **$300 credit** for 90 days (new accounts)
- **Always free** tier with monthly limits:
  - 1 f1-micro VM instance (US regions only)
  - 30GB of storage
  - 5GB of Cloud Storage
  - 2 million Cloud Functions invocations
  - BigQuery: 1TB of querying, 10GB of storage
  - And more...

### Requirements:
- Google account (Gmail or Google Workspace)
- Phone number for verification
- Credit/debit card (will NOT be charged without explicit upgrade)
- Valid billing address

### Step-by-Step Instructions:

**Step 1: Go to Google Cloud**
- Visit: https://cloud.google.com
- Click **"Get started for free"** or **"Try for free"**

**Step 2: Sign In with Google**
```
If you have a Gmail account:
- Sign in with your existing Google account

If you don't have a Google account:
- Click "Create account"
- Follow Google account creation process
- Then return to Google Cloud
```

**Step 3: Country and Terms**
```
Country: United States (or your country)
```
- ☑ Check: "I agree to the Google Cloud Platform Terms of Service"
- ☑ Check: Email updates (optional)
- Click **"Continue"**

**Step 4: Account Type**
```
Choose: Account type
○ Individual
○ Business

For learning: Choose Individual
```

**Step 5: Payment Information**
```
Name and address:
Full name: Your Name
Address line 1: Your address
City: Your city
State/Province: Your state
Postal code: Your ZIP code

Payment method:
Credit or debit card number: Your card number
Expiration date: MM/YY
CVC: 3-digit code
```
- Click **"Start my free trial"**

⚠️ **Important**: Google will charge $1-$2 to verify your card, then immediately refund it.

**Step 6: Verification**
- Google may ask you to verify your identity
- Follow the on-screen instructions
- This might include phone verification

**Step 7: Welcome to Google Cloud**
- You'll see the Google Cloud Console dashboard
- Your $300 credit is now active for 90 days

**Step 8: Create Your First Project (Optional)**
```
Project name: My First Project
Project ID: (auto-generated, can customize)
Location: No organization
```
- Click **"Create"**

### Important Notes:
- 💳 **Auto-upgrade**: GCP will NOT automatically charge you after free trial ends
- 📊 **Monitor credits**: Check remaining credits in billing dashboard
- 🔔 **Budget alerts**: Set up budget alerts for peace of mind
- 📚 **Free tier limits**: https://cloud.google.com/free

## Microsoft Azure Free Tier Account

### What You Get:
- **$200 credit** for 30 days (new accounts)
- **12 months free** on popular services:
  - 750 hours/month of B1S Windows or Linux virtual machines
  - 5GB of blob storage
  - 250GB of SQL Database
  - 1 million Azure Functions executions
- **Always free** services:
  - 25+ services always free (with limits)
  - App Service: 10 web/mobile/API apps
  - Azure Functions: 1 million requests/month
  - And more...

### Requirements:
- Microsoft account (Outlook, Hotmail, or create new)
- Phone number for verification
- Credit/debit card (will NOT be charged for free services)
- Valid identity verification

### Step-by-Step Instructions:

**Step 1: Go to Azure**
- Visit: https://azure.microsoft.com/free
- Click **"Start free"** or **"Try Azure for free"**

**Step 2: Sign In with Microsoft Account**
```
If you have a Microsoft account (Outlook, Hotmail, Xbox):
- Sign in with existing account

If you don't have a Microsoft account:
- Click "Create one"
- Create a new Microsoft account
- Then return to Azure signup
```

**Step 3: About You**
```
Country/Region: United States (or your country)
First name: Your first name
Last name: Your last name
Email address: (pre-filled from Microsoft account)
Phone: +1-234-567-8900
```
- Click **"Next"**

**Step 4: Identity Verification by Phone**
```
Verification method:
○ Text me
○ Call me

Enter phone number: Your mobile number
```
- Click **"Send verification code"** or **"Call me"**
- Enter the code you receive
- Click **"Verify code"**

**Step 5: Identity Verification by Card**
```
Cardholder name: Name on card
Card number: Your credit/debit card number
Expiration date: MM/YY
CVV: 3-4 digit security code

Billing address:
Address line 1: Your address
City: Your city
State/Province: Your state
Postal code: Your ZIP code
```
- Click **"Next"**

⚠️ **Important**: Azure will charge $1 to verify your card, then immediately refund it.

**Step 6: Agreement**
```
☑ I agree to the subscription agreement, offer details, and privacy statement
```
- Complete CAPTCHA verification
- Click **"Sign up"**

**Step 7: Welcome to Azure**
- Setup takes a few minutes
- You'll be redirected to the Azure Portal
- You'll see: "You're ready to start with Azure"

**Step 8: Quick Start (Optional)**
- Azure may offer a guided tour
- You can skip or follow along
- Click **"Go to the Azure portal"**

### Important Notes:
- 🔄 **Auto-renew**: By default, Azure does NOT auto-charge after free trial
- 📧 **Email verification**: Verify your email address
- 🔐 **Security**: Enable two-factor authentication
- 📊 **Cost management**: Set up budget alerts immediately
- 📚 **Free services**: https://azure.microsoft.com/free

## After Account Creation

### 1. Secure Your Account (All Providers)
- ✅ Enable Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA/2FA)
- ✅ Review and delete default access keys
- ✅ Set up billing alerts
- ✅ Review security recommendations

### 2. Set Up Billing Alerts

**AWS:**
```
1. Go to Billing Dashboard
2. Click "Billing preferences"
3. Check "Receive Free Tier Usage Alerts"
4. Enter email address
5. Set threshold (e.g., $10)
6. Save preferences
```

**Google Cloud:**
```
1. Go to Billing → Budgets & alerts
2. Click "Create budget"
3. Set budget amount (e.g., $50)
4. Set alert thresholds (50%, 90%, 100%)
5. Create
```

**Azure:**
```
1. Go to Cost Management + Billing
2. Click "Budgets"
3. Click "+ Add"
4. Set budget amount (e.g., $50)
5. Set alert conditions
6. Create
```

### 3. Explore the Console
- Familiarize yourself with the dashboard
- Browse available services
- Check the free tier dashboard
- Review documentation and tutorials

### 4. Start with Simple Projects
- Create a simple website
- Deploy a virtual machine
- Set up a database
- Try serverless functions

## Troubleshooting

### Common Issues:

**"Card declined"**
- Ensure card has at least $1-2 available for verification
- Try a different card
- Contact your bank (they may block cloud provider charges)

**"Phone number already in use"**
- Each phone number can typically only be used once per provider
- Try a different phone number
- Contact support

**"Verification failed"**
- Ensure all information matches your card exactly
- Check for typos in address
- Try again in a few hours

**"Can't receive verification code"**
- Check phone signal
- Try voice call instead of SMS
- Check spam folder for emails
- Wait a few minutes and try again

## Important Reminders

⚠️ **Free Tier Limits**
- Monitor your usage to stay within free tier
- Free tier expires after specified period
- Some services are always free (with limits)

⚠️ **Credit Card Holds**
- All providers charge $1-2 for verification
- This is refunded within 3-7 business days
- It's not an actual charge, just a hold

⚠️ **Account Security**
- Always enable MFA/2FA
- Never share your credentials
- Review access regularly
- Delete unused resources

⚠️ **Billing Alerts**
- Set up alerts immediately
- Don't wait until you're charged
- Monitor usage weekly
